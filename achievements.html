Accolades

The Seraphim has it's fair share of accolades from the Spirit World's creation to present day.

The main list of accolades has been listed here:

  • January 2012: Creation of the Spirit World.
  • July 2012: World-Ender defeated and contained by Seraph.
  • August 2013: Godbreaker defeated and contained.
  • April 2014-December 2014: Shadow Wars fought. 5,000 casualties.
  • June 2016: Base 13 destroyed in arson attack by Evelyn.
  • September 2017-March 2020: Hell Wars fought and won. 26,000 casualties. 13,000 deaths.
  • April 2022: Bounty Hunter agency formed as part of Operation: Nightmare.
