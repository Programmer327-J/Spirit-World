Base 27

Beginnings

Base 27 originally started as Base 13, before it was burned down back in 2016 by ex-Seraphim member, Evelyn.

When the base was created, it was originally a small warehouse, set with basic necessities. Over time, it grew into a full agency with barracks and office spaces for the members of the Seraphim.


Members:

Base 27 is currently inhabited by the main group, and around 15,000 soldiers. The main group consists of:

  • Seraph/Aeryx Rose: Leader of Base 27 and head of Delta Squad.
  • Katherine Whiterose: Accountant and Second-in-Command.
  • Ruby Brookhaven: Mission Delegator.
  • Delta 1-5: The main members of Delta Squad and the best of the best. Names have been expunged from Seraphim records due to anonymity.
  • Evelyn Brookhaven: Older sister of Ruby and manager of the armory.

Current status.

Base 27 has been the main area of operations since August 2016, and plays host to the most fun and the most interesting people this side of an external reality.
