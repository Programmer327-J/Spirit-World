Azeroth.

Who is Azeroth?

Azeroth, more commonly known as Azzy by his friends, is the representation of Seraph's anger, and acts as the bridge between control and a lack thereof.

Azeroth also gives Seraph the ability to glitch around, both in and out of reality, making them unpredicable.
