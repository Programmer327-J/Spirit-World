Welcome to the Spirit World.

What do we do?

The Spirit World was created as a haven for those who feel alone or without a place to go. I made the Spirit World in 2012 to cope with bullying and having no friends.


What has been achieved?

Since creation, the Spirit World has gone from a coping mechanism for loneliness to intergrations in ways i never thought possible such as:

  • DnD campaigns with friends.
  • Wattpad Stories.
  • Potential Cosplay outfits.
  • Realising my sexuality and gender.
