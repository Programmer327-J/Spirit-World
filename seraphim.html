The Seraphim.

What is the Seraphim?

The Seraphim is an elite agency, formed by Seraph back in 2012.

The Seraphim used to combat demons, however, financial problems caused the Seraphim to branch out into different groups.


Formation

The Seraphim was formed after a fight with an entity known as World-Ender back in 2012, and now garners 15,000 inter-dimensional personnel all in different departments such as:

  • Operations
  • Science
  • Bounty Hunting
  • Military
  • And Much More.
